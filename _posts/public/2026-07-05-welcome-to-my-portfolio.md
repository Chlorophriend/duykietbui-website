Hello and welcome! If you have visited this site in the past, you will notice that things look quite different now. 

After leaving this portfolio untouched for a while, I decided it was time for a complete visual and structural renovation. The old, heavy HTML5 UP theme has been replaced with a custom, high-performance, dark-mode-first aesthetic designed to load quickly and adapt beautifully to any screen size.

## What is New?

- **Custom-Crafted Design:** A modern grid-based layout utilizing CSS custom properties for seamless light and dark mode toggling.
- **Improved Performance:** I removed all legacy jQuery plugins and replaced them with a single, highly optimized Vanilla JS file (which cut down the page weight significantly).
- **Corrected Navigation & Layouts:** Dead links have been repaired, and there are now dedicated layout structures for articles and standard pages.
- **Interactive Academic Profile:** An elegant resume section directly on the home page with quick details about my research focus areas (Psychometrics, Sexuality, and DEI) and a direct PDF download option.

## Moving Forward

As an aspiring I/O Psychologist with a background in psychology and data science, I am working on merging statistical methodologies with human behavior studies. Moving forward, I plan to use this space to publish research summaries, statistics projects, and notes on topics like scale validation and organizational equity.
